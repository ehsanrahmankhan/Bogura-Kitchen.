All FAQ

To change your reservation details, please follow these steps:

  • Go to our website and sign in to your account.
  • Click on your profile picture in the top right corner and select "Reservations."
  • Find the reservation you want to change and click "Edit."
  • Make your changes and click on "Save."

Our payment system allows you to conveniently settle your bill through various methods, including credit cards, debit cards, and mobile payments. The process involves secure transactions that ensure your payment is processed efficiently.

To cancel your account, please follow these steps:

  • Go to our website and sign in to your account.
  • Click on your profile picture in the top right corner of the page and select "Account Settings."
  • Scroll to the bottom of the page and click on "Cancel Account."
  • Enter your password and click on "Cancel Account."
